Life at GDIT

Here we have a sense of ownership in our delivery, in our work, and in our career. We lead with integrity and compassion, get work done by collaborating and connecting with each other.
Our Culture

Your place to make a personal impact.

The work we do touches many. But what's more, is the impact the work has on us as individuals. We take pride in our achievements, big and small.

Work with meaning and purpose.

Whether it's supporting the safety and security of our nation, improving the health of our citizens or protecting the warfighter, you make a difference every day.

People-powered culture.

From work-life flexibility to building connection and recognizing great work, we take care of our people. Every decision we make has people at its center.

Own your career.

We grow and develop with the support of our colleagues and leaders. Our people grow their careers with GDIT and can go virtually anywhere.
